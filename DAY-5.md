**Day 5: Why your AI prompts give generic answers (and how to fix it)**

I ran the same prompt twice.
Same topic. Same AI. Completely different results.

**Prompt A:** "Create a 30-day learning roadmap."
→ Got a roadmap that could've been for anyone. Or no one.

**Prompt B:** Same request + my situation, skills, goal, time, and learning style.
→ Got a roadmap I could actually start tomorrow.

The difference? **Context.**

AI doesn't know you. It only knows what you tell it.

5 things that transform any prompt:
→ Who you are (student / professional / freelancer)
→ What you already know
→ What you're trying to achieve
→ How much time you have
→ How you learn best
